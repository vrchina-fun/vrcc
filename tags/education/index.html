Chinese Students Mobility Trends

  • Society

With a population of almost 1.4 billion and a burgeoning middle class, China sends more students abroad than any other country in the world. Around 460,000 Chinese students were enrolled in foreign educational institutions in 2014, a rise of eleven percent over the previous year.

Life of Confucius

Confucius has the greatest influence on Chinese characters. Besides being a great educationist, thinker, and unsuccessful politician, he was, first of all, an intellect with a noble morality. He pursued truth, kindness, and perfection throughout his life and his success and failure were largely due to his character, which had an everlasting impact on Chinese intellects.

The First Chinese Library in Mexico to Promote Cultural Exchange

  • Society
  • Education , Language

The first China-themed library in Mexico was inaugurated on Monday, April 24, 2017, in a bid to promote cultural exchange. Books on Chinese history, art, culture, traditional medicine and music, among other topics, as well as digital and video archives, can be found at the new Chinese Library at Mexico City’s Anahuac University.
