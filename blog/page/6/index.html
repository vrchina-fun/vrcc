Crested Ibis

  • Species
  • Nature , Animal

The crested ibis is a rare and precious species in the world. In history, crested ibises were widely distributed in East Asia. Since the middle of the 20th century, its population has reduced rapidly due to the change of the inadaptable environment caused by human activities, such as the decrease of the rice fields, the wide applications of fertilizers and pesticides and the reduction of forest area.
