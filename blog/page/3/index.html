China's Political System 

  • Society
  • Encyclopedia

The people’s congress system is China’s fundamental political system. The Constitution of the People’s Republic of China (PRC) provides that state power belongs to the people. The National People’s Congress (NPC) and local people’s congresses at all levels are organs representing the people in exercising state power.

Chinese Alcohol History and Culture

  • Culture
  • Food

In China, alcohol is also called the “Water of History”, because the stories of alcohol can be traced back to almost every period in Chinese history. A legend says that Yi Di, the wife of the first Dynasty’s emperor Yu invented the method to make alcohol In ancient China, since alcohol was regarded as a sacred liquid, only when people made sacrificial offerings to the Heaven and the Earth or ancestors was it used.

Chinese Embroidery

  • Art
  • Craft

The earliest examples of Chinese embroidery stem from the Zhou Dynasty. These might well represent the origin of embroidery, but early examples of which have also been discovered in ancient Egypt and northern Europe, though there was no conclusive proof of exactly where embroidery originated.
